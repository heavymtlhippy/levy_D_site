Dori is a highly experienced New Jersey state-certified Learning Consultant with over 45 years of relevant experience. She has worked in Special Education for over X years, as a teacher, student case manager at all grade levels, Director of Special Education, and School Administrator. She has also worked as a Learning Consultant in the NJ school districts of Palmyra and Medford Lakes. Dori's goal is to assist parents by helping them collaborate with the school district to develop a personalized education plan for their children based on their unique needs.
She is a certified New Jersey LTDC (Learning Disabilities Teacher Consultant)
